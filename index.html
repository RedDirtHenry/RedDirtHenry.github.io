About Me

Below are some highlights from my education, hobbies, and professional experiences.

Paris, France

Currently based in Paris, France. I have experience studying and working in the US, China, India, Sweden, and France, and work well in international and diverse environments.

Nuclear Engineer

Hold a double Masters degree in Nuclear Engineering and Material Science from KTH Royal Institute of Technology and École Nationale Supérieure de Physique, Électronique et Matériaux. My master's thesis was on the numerical stability of thermal-hydraulic modeling in the MAAP nuclear accident code.

Data Scientist

Full-Stack data scientist, handling data pipeline, ETL, modeling, and business reporting. Currently working in demand forecast modeling and numerical optimizations for Aeronautics and e-Commerce businesses.

Programming

Enjoy experimenting and building projects in different programming languages including: Python, R, Julia, C#, and JavaScript. Projects range from web development to machine learning to data visualization.

Texas A&M Aggie

Graduated in 2017 with a BSc. in Nuclear Engineering with a minor in mathematics from Texas A&M Univeristy. Was a member of the American Nuclear Society, Health Physics Society, and Phi Eta Sigma national honor society. Also obtained my Process Safety Engineering Certificate from the Mary Kay O'Connor Safety Center.

Languages

English is my native language, I can speak, read, and write at a business professional level in French, and know the basic notions of Arabic.

Te Araroa 2018

Completed the southern section of the Te Araroa, a ~1400km thru-hiking trail in New Zealand that traverses the entire southern island.
